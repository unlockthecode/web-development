The Little Taco Shop

Welcome to the Little Taco Shop, where we serve the most delicious and authentic tacos in town! Our mission is to provide a cozy atmosphere where you can enjoy fresh, flavorful tacos made with love and the finest ingredients. Whether you're a fan of classic flavors or adventurous combinations, we have something for everyone. Come visit us and experience the taste of Mexico right in your neighborhood!

Taco Trivias

Expand for Taco Trivias
  • The word "taco" means "light lunch" in Spanish.
  • Tacos are believed to have originated in Mexico in the 18th century.
  • The first taco truck in the United States was established in Los Angeles in the early 1900s.
  • Tacos can be made with a variety of fillings, including beef, chicken, pork, fish, and vegetables.
  • National Taco Day is celebrated on October 4th each year in the United States.
